HYBRID.NETWORK

A Global Hybrid-Blockchain Open-Source Community

Hybrid.Network is a global, independent group of open source developers, designers and translators formed to support the cores and ecosystems for hybrid consensus mechanism blockchains.

Principles

The community is free to choose its own targets and objectives. That said, our goal is to improve hybrid blockchains, so we will align with those team's needs and avoid retreading old ground as much as possible, unless it is possible to attain a substantially higher level of quality.

no 1

Projects will be inclusive to all persons regardless of any personal differences.

no 2

Funding details will be open and transparent to the community.

no 3

Any member of Hybrid.Network can create and collaborate on projects.

no 4

Contributions including finding bugs, writing code and community engagement are rewarded with bounties when possible.

no 5

Bi-Weekly reports to the community will be made and are an important part of maintaining transparency.

no 6

Community and organizational governance will be provided by decentralized consensus when possible.
